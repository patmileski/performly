Development that works for every person

Everything your team
needs to grow together

CoachOps360 gives every team member a clear path forward — with goals they understand, coaching they can reference, and progress they can see. Not just at review time. Every week.

🎯

Goals & Key Results

Weekly, monthly, and annual goals with trackable key results. Overdue goals are automatically flagged with red indicators so nothing slips through the cracks.

📅

1:1 Scheduling + Calendar Export

Schedule sessions, build agendas, and let team members add their own talking points. Export any session directly to Outlook or Google Calendar with one click.

📝

Rich Coaching Notes

Log notes with a full rich-text editor — headings, tables, bullet lists, font colors, highlights. Edit or delete any note. Full coaching history per person, always searchable.

✅

Task Tracking with Overdue Alerts

Assign tasks with due dates and priority. Overdue tasks are flagged automatically. Team members check off tasks and add progress notes from their own private dashboard.

📄

Annual Review Uploads

Upload performance reviews as PDF or Word to each person's profile. Team members can view and download their own reviews anytime — no shared drives or lost emails.

👤

Private Team Member Portal

Each person logs in with a PIN to their own private dashboard — goals, 1:1s, coaching notes, tasks, reviews, and job description. Private to them, always available.

⚠️

Overdue Goal & Task Indicators

Past-due goals and tasks are automatically highlighted with overdue counts on the dashboard. Always know what needs attention before your next coaching conversation.

💼

Job Descriptions

Store each team member's job description in their profile so they can always reference their role, responsibilities, and what great performance looks like.

⚙️

Account & Subscription Management

Update your profile, change plans, and manage billing — all from inside the app. Cancel or upgrade anytime without contacting support.
